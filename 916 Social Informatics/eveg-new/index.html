1. How to Browse Products?

Scroll down the homepage to explore our products.

2. How to Use the Search Function?

Enter keywords in the search bar on the homepage to show relevant products.

3. How to Filter Search Results?

Go to "Filter" dropdown menu to narrow down results based on category.

4. How to Sort Search Results?

Go to "Sort by" dropdown menu to organize and view search results based on your preference for product name or price order.

5. How to Add Items to the Basket?

To add items to your basket, you can click the "+" button below a product to add one unit, and optionally click the "-" button to reduce the quantity by one. You may also enter the quantity in the input field below the product.

6. How to View and Edit the Basket?

Click on "Basket" in the top right corner to view the basket, you can increase or decrease the quantity of items, remove a product or clear the basket.

7. How to Make a Payment?

Click on "Checkout" from the Basket to proceed to checkout page, where you can browse your order and make further adjustments. Once you have confirmed the shopping list, enter payment details and then click "Pay".

InterVeg Coventry

InterVeg Coventry Ltd. is a devoted enterprise committed to delivering a bountiful array of fresh produce harvested from the verdant landscapes of the Midlands.

Established in the year 2021, our mission revolves around the delivery of superior-quality fruits and vegetables that are sourced locally. We aspire to bring the essence of the Midlands' lush countryside directly to our customers, ensuring a delightful and nourishing experience with each product we offer, cultivating a greener, healthier future, uniting tradition and innovation in every harvest.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us. Thank you for choosing our services, and we look forward to assisting you.
